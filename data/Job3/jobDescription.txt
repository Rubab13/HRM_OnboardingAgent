JOB TITLE: AI Developer (RAG Architecture)

COMPANY NAME: IntelliSystems AI Inc.

LOCATION: Remote (USA & Canada)  
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-time  
DEPARTMENT: AI Engineering & Research  
REPORTS TO: VP of AI and Machine Learning

ABOUT THE COMPANY  
IntelliSystems AI Inc. is an innovative artificial intelligence company specializing in large language model applications and retrieval-augmented generation systems for enterprise clients. We develop cutting-edge AI solutions that help organizations leverage their knowledge bases, improve decision-making, and enhance customer experiences through intelligent automation.

POSITION SUMMARY  
IntelliSystems AI Inc. is seeking an AI Developer with expertise in RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture to design and implement advanced AI systems that combine large language models with efficient information retrieval. This role involves building scalable RAG pipelines, optimizing vector databases, and creating intelligent applications that provide accurate, context-aware responses.

The ideal candidate has strong experience with LLMs, vector databases, embeddings, and modern AI frameworks, with a passion for pushing the boundaries of what's possible with retrieval-augmented generation systems.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES  
• Design and implement RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems using LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar frameworks  
• Develop and optimize vector databases using Pinecone, Weaviate, or ChromaDB for efficient semantic search  
• Build data ingestion pipelines for processing and chunking documents from various sources  
• Create and fine-tune embedding models for optimal retrieval performance  
• Integrate large language models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) with retrieval systems  
• Implement prompt engineering strategies and context window optimization  
• Develop evaluation metrics and testing frameworks for RAG system performance  
• Optimize retrieval strategies including hybrid search, re-ranking, and query expansion  
• Build APIs and microservices for RAG applications using FastAPI or Flask  
• Monitor and improve system accuracy, latency, and cost efficiency  

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS  
• Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning, or related field  
• 3–5 years of experience in AI/ML development with focus on NLP and LLMs  
• Strong hands-on experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar RAG frameworks  
• Proficiency in Python and ML libraries (PyTorch, TensorFlow, Transformers)  
• Experience with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus, ChromaDB)  
• Understanding of embedding models, semantic search, and information retrieval  
• Experience with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini)  
• Knowledge of document processing and chunking strategies  
• Strong problem-solving and optimization skills  

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS  
• Experience with fine-tuning LLMs and custom embedding models  
• Knowledge of advanced RAG techniques (HyDE, multi-query, fusion retrieval)  
• Familiarity with graph databases and knowledge graphs  
• Experience with MLOps practices and model deployment  
• Understanding of responsible AI, hallucination mitigation, and safety  
• Prior experience building production-grade AI applications
