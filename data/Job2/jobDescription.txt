JOB TITLE: Data Analyst

COMPANY NAME: Apex Analytics Corp.

LOCATION: Hybrid (Toronto, ON & New York, NY)  
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-time  
DEPARTMENT: Business Intelligence & Analytics  
REPORTS TO: Head of Data Analytics

ABOUT THE COMPANY  
Apex Analytics Corp. is a leading data consulting firm specializing in business intelligence, predictive analytics, and data-driven decision-making for enterprise clients across North America. We help organizations unlock insights from their data to drive growth and operational excellence.

POSITION SUMMARY  
Apex Analytics Corp. is seeking a Data Analyst to join our Business Intelligence team. This role involves analyzing complex datasets, creating reports and dashboards, and providing actionable insights to stakeholders. The ideal candidate will work closely with business teams to understand requirements and translate data into meaningful stories.

The successful candidate has strong analytical skills, experience with data visualization tools, and the ability to communicate findings effectively to both technical and non-technical audiences.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES  
• Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights  
• Develop and maintain interactive dashboards and reports using Tableau and Power BI  
• Write SQL queries to extract and transform data from relational databases  
• Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand analytical requirements  
• Perform statistical analysis and A/B testing to support business decisions  
• Clean and validate data to ensure accuracy and consistency  
• Create data visualizations and presentations for executive leadership  
• Document analytical processes and data definitions  
• Support data quality initiatives and data governance activities  
• Stay current with analytics tools and industry best practices  

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS  
• Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, or related field  
• 2–3 years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, or related role  
• Strong proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation  
• Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI  
• Proficiency in Microsoft Excel including pivot tables, VLOOKUP, and formulas  
• Understanding of statistical concepts and methods  
• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills  
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills  

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS  
• Experience with Python or R for statistical analysis  
• Knowledge of data warehousing concepts and ETL processes  
• Familiarity with Google Analytics or similar web analytics platforms  
• Experience with A/B testing and experimental design  
• Prior experience in consulting or client-facing roles
